Oryx Cows & Sheep Farm

Founded in Al Ain in 2018, Oryx Farm is a leading Emirati company specializing in cattle and sheep farming. The farm provides a wide range of services and products to serve all categories, including individuals, restaurants, companies, and markets. Under Emirati management, the farm is committed to the highest standards of livestock care and product quality. Oryx Farm plays an active role in supporting food security and delivering safe, healthy products to the community. Its vision combines traditional livestock expertise with modern practices to ensure the best outcomes.
